Frequently Asked Questions

How do I switch between Day and Night?

Tap anywhere on the playing field (not on a UI button) to swap worlds. On keyboard (iPad with Magic Keyboard): Space or Enter.

How do I reset a level?

Tap the circular arrow icon (↺) at the top right of the screen. Your progress on previous levels is not affected.

What do the stars mean?

Each level has a "minimum moves" target and "minimum swaps" target. Completing the level earns you 1 star. Solving in minimum moves earns a 2nd star. Solving in minimum swaps earns the 3rd star. Stars are persistent — you keep your best rating even if a future replay is worse.

I'm stuck on a level. Can I skip it?

The level select screen (≡ menu top left) shows all unlocked levels — you can replay any completed level. To unlock the next level, the previous one must be completed at least once.

Do you collect any personal data?

No. The game does not require an account, does not track you, and stores all progress locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Where can I change settings?

Tap the hamburger menu (≡) at the top left → Settings tab. From there you can toggle audio, choose a visual theme, or open the leaderboards.

The leaderboard isn't showing my scores

Leaderboards require Game Center to be enabled. In iOS Settings → Game Center, make sure you're signed in. Re-open the game; your scores will sync automatically on the next level completion.

Bug reports

If something goes wrong, the most helpful info is:

  • Your iPhone / iPad model and iOS version
  • Which level you were on
  • Which theme was active
  • What you did right before the bug appeared
  • A screenshot if possible

Send these to the email above — we'll get back to you.

Feature requests

We're actively developing the game. If there's a mechanic, theme, or feature you'd love to see, drop us an email or open an issue on GitHub. Player input directly shapes the roadmap.
